Cook County, IL government data
Data.CookCountyIL.Gov
Data.CookCountyIL.Gov is the web portal for the Cook County, Illinois Government. Data related to courts, economic development, health care, public safety, and more, is available through the website and API.

Data.CookCountyIL.Gov uses the Socrata Open Data API, which supports a RESTful protocol and returns responses in a variety of formats including JSON and XML.
