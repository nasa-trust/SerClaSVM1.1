Hotwire Hotel Shopping
The Hotel Shopping API delivers data describing hotel shopping results similar to those that can be obtained when shopping for hotel rates on  Hotwire.com. Each result returned includes a URL that can be used to view additional details and complete a purchase on Hotwire.com.
