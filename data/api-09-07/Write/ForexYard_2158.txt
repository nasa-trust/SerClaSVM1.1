Automated Trading Service
ForexYard
ForexYard is an online brokerage providing to services to traders around the world. The ForexYard API enables users to build automated trading into third-party systems. Possibly trading actions include: trade requests, stop-loss, take-profit, entry orders, access to past trading, historical data requests, and charting.  The use the API, users must agree to the API disclosure and maintain sufficient funds in trading accounts.
