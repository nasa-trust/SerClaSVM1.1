Energy consumption and supply information service
U.K National Grid Energy Flow
The service provides updated and time-stamped reports of electricity and natural gas usage across its service areas in the United Kingdom and the northeastern United States. It updates usage data every 12 minutes to generate a report of energy demand and the provider&#039;s environmental performance.

API methods support retrieval of energy flow data along with the timestamp of the most recent update. Data are provided in compressed format.
