City of Chicago Data Portal
The City of Chicago Data Portal is just that, an online portal to data for the City of Chicago. Datasets are available for categories including administration and finance; education; historic preservation; parks and recreation; sanitation; transportation; and more. 

The City of Chicago Data Portal uses the Socrata Open Data API, which uses REST and returns responses in a variety of formats including JSON, XML, and RDF.
