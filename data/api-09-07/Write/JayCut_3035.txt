Online video editing service
JayCut
JayCut is an online based video editing service. The JayCut API allows users to integrate the video editing services into their websites. The API exposes the following functionality:

*Embedding the editing tool on your site
*Authorization of users
*Upload of media, tied to users
*Management of preset content
*Streaming of video
*Export of edited videos, out of the JayCut system

The API uses RESTful calls and responses are formatted in XML.
