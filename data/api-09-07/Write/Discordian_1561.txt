Date converter from Gregorian to POEE calendar
Discordian
The Discordian API converts dates from the Gregorian calendar to the Semi-Divinely Revealed POEE calendar. The Gregorian calendar (also called the Western or Christian calendar) is the internationally accepted civil calendar. The Semi-Divinely Revealed POEE calendar is used by adherents of the Discordian religion. The Discordian API operates over SOAP protocols.
