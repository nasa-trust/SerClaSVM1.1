Video management and publishing platform 
Framesocket
Framesocket is a video hosting and publishing platform. Framesocket works with websites, tablets, and mobiles. Framesocket also offers analytics about video consumption and visitors.

The Framesocket API allows developers to access and integrate Framesocket&#039;s functionality into other applications and services. Some example API methods include integrating videos into web, phone, and tablet applications, playing videos, measuring analytics and traffic, and managing accounts.
