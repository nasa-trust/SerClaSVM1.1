Online collaboration and project management service
Simprove Workity
The service headquartered in Germany provides task management, scheduling, messaging, and related functions for online project management and collaboration within a project team. Services supported include adding and assigning team members, creating tasks with due date and other scheduling functions, posting notes, tagging objects with keywords and task relationships, etc.

Methods handle functions like work item retrieval and updating as well as user account lookup and update. Task-related methods support ticket creation, assignment, updating, and tracking.
