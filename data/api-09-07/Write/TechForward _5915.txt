TechForward 
TechForward is a tech marketplace. It offers consumers and merchants a streamlined system for updating electronic gadgetry. Merchants can subscribe to a network that allows them to make use of trade-in technology. Consumers can find plans with willing merchants that provide guaranteed prices for trade-ins.
