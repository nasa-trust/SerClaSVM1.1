JacTravel is a tourism and hospitality services provider. With an extensive network of hospitality-related enterprises around the globe, JacTravel offers a wide range of lodging solutions. They inspect their partners??? locations individually and help tour operators and retailers find and book the most appropriate lodgings. Their API allows retailers to integrate JacTravel searches directly into their websites. The API returns XML-encoded results.
