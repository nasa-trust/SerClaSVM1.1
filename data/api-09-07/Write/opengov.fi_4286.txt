Finland open government data
opengov.fi
opengov.fi provides open government data for Finland. The site works closely with suomi.fi, the Finnish government services portal, for formal data cataloging purposes. Available datasets include environmental and spatial services information; transportation; events; maps; and more. 

opengov.fi is powered by CKAN (Comprehensive Knowledge Archive Network) and data is accessible through the RESTful CKAN API.
