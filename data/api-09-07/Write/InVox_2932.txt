Small and medium-sized business phone service
InVox
Cloud-based service supporting business phone systems for small and medium-sized companies. The web-based phone designer tool allows users to build business telephone systems and advanced IVR systems without installing any new software or hardware using a visual drag-and-drop interface without programming. Data integration capabilities include real-time data exchange. Applications can scale to handle tens of simultaneous calls.
