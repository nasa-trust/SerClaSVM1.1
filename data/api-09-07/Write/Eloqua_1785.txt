Eloqua
Eloqua is a platform for businesses interested in automated marketing and reporting services. The Eloqua platform provides services to aid in campaign execution, testing, measurement, prospect profiling, and lead nurturing.

The Eloqua API is a robust SOAP-based web service that allows programmatic interactions with the Eloqua platform. The services provided by the API allow users to integrate their existing systems with the Eloqua platform and manage a variety of assets and data stores that are accumulated in platform&#039;s data store.
